# blinky_board_crown
Blinky board created to look like a crown; the jems are represented by led lights. I made this as a starter project, in which I want to present it either as a gift to my friend or as a display at my engineering pit. The inspiration for it came from my FRC team, Ladytalons, as our whole thing is that we wear crowns and love sparkly purple. To mimic this theme, the final PCB is designed to crown as stated before, with the PCB being manufactured in a purple color and the gems/led lights being white. The design/schematic of the PCB allows the LEDs to blink alternately, creating the "sparkle" aspect of the theme.
